uSave is an application that is going to help you control your savings. Nowadays every person has certain needs and desires. It is very tough for many people to put priorities in their lives. Many people spend their money thoughtlessly and at the end of the month they are penniless. In 2013 it is too hard for someone to save money in order to buy something that is really useful for him or to invest them. If you are among those people, if you can not control your savings, if you want to realize where you spend you your whole salary at the end of the month, then uSave is the suitable application for you.

Scan your receipts or take a photo of them.Upload all your receipts in order to store your outgoing in database.Insert your monthly salary and any other income you may have.At the end of the week, month, year you can learn what your savings are and in which consuming sector you have spent more money. Now it is easier than ever to control your savings and find out where you spend more money.

FAQ

How can i store my receipts in database?
You should simple take a photo of your receipt and upload to your account?
Is my outgoing categorized?
After you upload your receipt, then the data are categorized. As a result you know the category that you have spent more money for.
